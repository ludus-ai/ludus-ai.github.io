How it Works

Ludus AI is an interactive teacher that uses the latest artificial intelligence technology to provide personalized education to its users. Here's how it works:

  1. Try the prototype.
  2. Tell us about yourself and your interests.
  3. Then just start talking! Your AI teacher will adapt based on your interests and also your grade, if you mention it.
  4. Ask it for anything. Ludus AI is knowledgable on lots of subjects, and is designed specifically for students.
  5. Learn at your own pace. Ask questions. Learn more. Repeat.
  6. As you learn, the AI will adapt to your specific type of learning.
  7. Use natural language. If you want to know something, ask it.
